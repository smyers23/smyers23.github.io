Space Politics Duality

Welcome to The Space Politics Duality! This is the personal website of Sam Myers, current planetary sciences graduate student at the University of Arizona. Here you can learn about me, my research with NEAs, and some of my other projects.

I recently migrated this site from Wordpress to custom html hosted on Github, so please bare with me if there are any weird typos of bugs. If you find one, I'd really appreciate if you brought it to my attention too!

The unfortunate reality of being a graduate student is that I am busyTM, and so I don't have a lot of time to dedicate to this whole "having a website" thing. So, please don't be surprised if its a little janky in some places, and if things are out of date.

If you want to get in contact with me you can reach me at sammyers -at- arizona.edu or message me on LinkedIn @samamyers. I'm happy to talk space, politics, D&D, or really anything else!
