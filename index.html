Space Politics Duality

Hello, my name is Sam Myers! I am a planetary sciences graduate student at the University of Arizona. My science work is focused on understanding near-Earth asteroids, but I also do work on science outreach and science policy. On this website you can take a look at the various projects I've undertaken, both professional and personal. These projects include my work to create a science policy fellowship in the state of the Arizona, my scientific publications, and my work with the conlang Toki Pona.

If you want to get in contact with me you can reach me at sammyers -at- arizona.edu or message me on LinkedIn @samamyers. I'm happy to talk space, policy, D&D, cross stitching, or anything else related to my work/interests.
