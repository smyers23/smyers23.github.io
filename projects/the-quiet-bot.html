The Quiet Bot

Originally Posted: October 15, 2025

Recently, I tried to play one of my favorite TTRPGs, The Quiet Year by Avery Alder, with some friends online. However, to my surprise, there wasn't a great method for playing the game via Discord. So, I decided to create my own Discord bot to aid in playing the game. If you'd like to try it out, you can add it to your Discord servers here!
